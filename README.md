## Project Overview
This project analyzes the increasing trend of online financial frauds in India, including UPI scams, phishing attacks, OTP frauds, and digital payment-related cybercrimes.

## Objectives
- Study major online financial fraud trends in India
- Identify causes behind digital financial frauds
- Analyze the impact on consumers and businesses
- Examine cybersecurity measures and fraud prevention methods

## Key Areas Covered
- UPI & Banking Frauds
- Phishing & OTP Scams
- Identity Theft
- Cybersecurity Measures
- Digital Payment Security
- Government Regulations

## Skills Used
- Data Analysis
- Fraud Detection & Prevention
